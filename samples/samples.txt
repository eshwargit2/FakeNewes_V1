Fake news examples

“Scientists confirm drinking hot lemon water every 30 minutes kills all cancer cells in 48 hours.”
“Government announces all citizens will receive $10,000 by tonight if they forward this message to 20 people.”
“NASA says Earth will be in total darkness for 6 days next month due to a rare cosmic event.”
“A celebrity was arrested for running an international money-laundering ring, according to an unnamed source.”
“Hospitals are hiding a simple 2-ingredient cure for diabetes because it would destroy the pharmaceutical industry.”

“The central bank kept interest rates unchanged in its latest policy review, citing stable inflation trends.”
“The health ministry reported an increase in seasonal flu cases and advised vaccination for high-risk groups.”
“A regional court ordered a temporary halt to the infrastructure project pending environmental review.”
